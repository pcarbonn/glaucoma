Glaucoma-Test Online

Use this page to find blind spots in your visual field with your PC or Mac.
This test is time-consuming, and doctors often don't give you enough time to do it.

Look at the small red dot in the middle of the screen with only one eye, fixedly.
Move the large red dot with the mouse or arrow keys (and Shift key) and search for blind spots.
Click the mouse when you don't see the large dot while fixing the small dot. Move and repeat.
Press the Enter key to view the blind zones in your visual field.
Print the web page and show it to your doctor.
Reload the page to restart.
